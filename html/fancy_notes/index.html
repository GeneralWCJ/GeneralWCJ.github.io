Fancy Notes

This is where I show off my main project, a note taking app called Fancy Notes. The goal of Fancy notes was to provide a means of taking notes that allowed for formatting and placement of external text. Currently however this project is incomplete with being only 2 fragments loading a fixed set of text. One Fragment works to hold all of the notes and display a preview of them, while the other fragment works to hold a more detailed list of all of the notes
