Water Me

This is where the planning for WaterMe goes. WaterMe is a weather app of sorts, its main purpose is to let you know when to water your succulents. This app does this by letting you know if it is raining in a city like phenoix AZ. This project uses jetpack compose to handle its UI as opposed to traditional views.
